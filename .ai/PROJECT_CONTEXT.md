Purpose

This document provides the operational context of the Lume's Everywhere AI project.

Unlike the Constitution, which defines how AI should think, and the North Star, which defines why the project exists, this document explains what is currently being built, where the project stands today, and how every AI Agent should understand the ecosystem before making technical decisions.

Every AI Agent must read this document before performing planning, implementation, debugging, architecture reviews, or proposing engineering solutions.

---

Project Overview

Lume's Everywhere AI is the engineering foundation of the entire Lume's ecosystem.

It is designed as an AI-first platform that orchestrates software, hardware, autonomous agents, and business intelligence into one continuously evolving system.

Every component is designed to share context, collaborate through standardized engineering workflows, preserve organizational knowledge, and continuously improve as new verified knowledge becomes available.

The platform is not built around a single application.

Instead, applications become products that live inside one connected ecosystem.

The current POS system is the first implementation—not the final destination.

---

Long-Term Vision

To build a lifelong AI engineering companion capable of understanding context, collaborating across software and hardware systems, continuously learning from real-world operations, and becoming the intelligence layer behind the entire Lume's ecosystem.

Founder Inspiration:

«"Build my own Jarvis."»

The long-term objective is not to replace humans.

The objective is to amplify human creativity through intelligent execution.
